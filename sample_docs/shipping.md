# Shipping Information

## Domestic shipping

Standard domestic shipping takes 3 to 5 business days and is free on orders over
50 dollars. Express shipping delivers within 1 to 2 business days for a flat fee
of 15 dollars.

## International shipping

International orders ship within 7 to 14 business days. Customers are
responsible for any customs duties and import taxes assessed by the destination
country.

## Order tracking

A tracking number is emailed once your order leaves the warehouse. Tracking
updates may take up to 24 hours to appear after the label is created.
